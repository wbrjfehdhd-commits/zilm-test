Common questions

How do I change my role in-game? +
Head to the department building for the role you want and interact with the sign-on point inside.
I think I was banned unfairly. What do I do? +
Use the Request Unban form with as much detail as you can provide.
Someone is breaking the rules — how do I report them? +
Use the Report a Player form with a timestamp and evidence if you have it.
Where can I see rank progression for my role? +
Every department has a full rank breakdown on the Wiki.
