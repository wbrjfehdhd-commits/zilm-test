Get Into Zilm

Play Now on Roblox

Emergency Zilm runs live inside Roblox. Follow the three steps below to get from your browser to the streets of Zilm.

▶ Launch on Roblox ↗ Read the Wiki first
How it works

Three steps to your first shift

①
Open Roblox
Have the Roblox app installed, or open it in your browser and sign in to your account.
②
Search Emergency Zilm
Use the Roblox search bar to find the Emergency Zilm experience and click Play.
③
Pick your role
Spawn in as a citizen, or head to a role-specific building to sign on as Police, Fire, EMS, or Mechanic.
New here? The Wiki covers controls, rank progression, and etiquette for every department.
